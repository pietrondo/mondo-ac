<Specification: Monster Footstep Particles>
## Description
Defines ground dust particles generated by heavy monster variants during traversal.

## Requirements
1. **Heavy Footstep Detection**:
   - Heavy monster variants (Brute, Golem) MUST trigger a footstep event at fixed walking intervals.
2. **Dust Emission**:
   - Upon a footstep event, the monster MUST spawn grey/brown dust particles at its foot position on the ground.

## Scenarios
### Scenario 1: Golem footstep dust spawn
* **Given** a heavy Golem monster is moving on the ground
* **When** its walking interval triggers a step
* **Then** dust particles MUST be spawned at its feet coordinates
